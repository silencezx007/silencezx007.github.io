## Idea 1: "The Glass Monolith"
**Design Movement**: Neumorphism meets Prism-punk.
**Core Principles**:
1.  **Transparency as Depth**: The interface is a series of stacked glass layers. Zen mode is clear, polished glass; Cyberpunk mode is shattered, refractive, and chromatic.
2.  **Light as Information**: Light sources dictate active states. Soft diffuse light for Zen, harsh neon lasers for Cyberpunk.
3.  **Fluidity**: Transitions are liquid-like, not mechanical.
**Color Philosophy**:
*   *Zen*: "Morning Fog" - Cool greys, soft whites, pale blues. Calm, clarity, breath.
*   *Cyberpunk*: "Neon Rain" - Deep void blacks, electric magentas, cyan lasers. High contrast, energy, stimulation.
**Layout Paradigm**: **The Floating Stack**. No rigid grid. Tools float in a masonry-style cloud. The "Dock" is a floating pill at the bottom, not a fixed bar.
**Signature Elements**:
1.  **Prismatic Borders**: Card borders that split light (rainbow gradients) on hover in Cyberpunk mode.
2.  **The Halo**: A subtle glow behind the active tool in Zen mode.
**Interaction Philosophy**: "Touch the Light". Interactions feel like interrupting light beams. Hover effects are luminous.
**Animation**:
*   *Zen*: Slow, breathing fades. Gaussian blur transitions.
*   *Cyberpunk*: Glitch effects, chromatic aberration on hover, snap-cuts.
**Typography System**:
*   *Headings*: **Space Grotesk** (Geometric, quirky).
*   *Body*: **Inter** (Clean, legible - *Note: Used sparingly, mostly for tool utility*).
*   *Code/Tech*: **JetBrains Mono** (For the "Geek" aesthetic).

## Idea 2: "The Binary Garden"
**Design Movement**: Organic Brutalism / Solarpunk vs. Cyber-Industrial.
**Core Principles**:
1.  **Nature vs. Machine**: Zen mode is organic, soft, and growing. Cyberpunk mode is raw, industrial, and constructed.
2.  **Structure as Art**: The grid is visible and celebrated in Cyberpunk mode, hidden and implied in Zen mode.
3.  **Tactility**: Buttons feel physical. Switches click.
**Color Philosophy**:
*   *Zen*: "Moss & Stone" - Sage greens, warm sand, slate greys. Grounded, natural, restorative.
*   *Cyberpunk*: "Circuit Board" - Dark slate, copper orange, terminal green. Functional, raw, engineered.
**Layout Paradigm**: **The Modular Grid**. A strict, visible grid system. In Zen mode, the grid lines fade, and content flows across cells. In Cyberpunk mode, grid lines light up and compartmentalize data.
**Signature Elements**:
1.  **The Toggle**: A massive, skeuomorphic industrial switch to change modes.
2.  **Data Veins**: Subtle lines connecting related tools in Cyberpunk mode.
**Interaction Philosophy**: "Mechanical Precision". Clicks have weight. Toggles have resistance.
**Animation**:
*   *Zen*: Unfolding, growing (like leaves). Ease-out curves.
*   *Cyberpunk*: Sliding, locking into place. Mechanical easing.
**Typography System**:
*   *Headings*: **Syne** (Artistic, wide).
*   *Body*: **DM Sans** (Humanist, approachable).
*   *Accents*: **Share Tech Mono** (Digital, retro).

## Idea 3: "The Ethereal Terminal"
**Design Movement**: Minimalist Futurism / HUD Interfaces.
**Core Principles**:
1.  **Content is King**: The UI recedes entirely. The tools are the heroes.
2.  **Focus vs. Chaos**: Zen mode isolates the task. Cyberpunk mode connects everything to the network.
3.  **Speed**: Everything is designed for rapid access (keyboard first).
**Color Philosophy**:
*   *Zen*: "Paper & Ink" - Off-white background, deep ink blue text. High contrast, academic, focused.
*   *Cyberpunk*: "The Matrix" - Deep purple background, holographic teal and pink data streams. Immersive, infinite.
**Layout Paradigm**: **The Command Center**. A central focus area for the active tool, surrounded by peripheral data streams that vanish in Zen mode.
**Signature Elements**:
1.  **The Command Line**: The search bar is always present and acts as the "brain".
2.  **Holographic Cards**: Tools look like 2D projections in Zen, 3D holograms in Cyberpunk.
**Interaction Philosophy**: "Thought Speed". Instant feedback. Keyboard shortcuts trigger visual ripples.
**Animation**:
*   *Zen*: Instant appearance with subtle opacity shifts.
*   *Cyberpunk*: Scanlines, decoding text effects, 3D rotations.
**Typography System**:
*   *Headings*: **Orbitron** (Futuristic, strong).
*   *Body*: **Rajdhani** (Squared, technical).
*   *Mono*: **Fira Code** (Developer standard).
